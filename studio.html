The Studio

We do the upstream work.

House of Ideas is a venture studio — not a fund, not an accelerator, not an incubator. We research domains, identify ideas with genuine market potential, build the execution roadmap, and then find the right operator to run it.

01 — What we are

A house, not a hatchery.

We bring the idea, the capital, the plan, and the operational scaffolding. The Fellow brings execution, instinct, and the hunger to build something real. The arrangement is intentional — both sides know what they signed up for.

We are not waiting for founders to pitch. We are not writing cheques and disappearing. We build alongside, in the open, with structure.

We are

  • Considered
  • Selective
  • Builder-first
  • Editorial
  • Patient

We are not

  • Hype-driven
  • Exclusive-for-its-own-sake
  • A fund
  • An accelerator
  • Slow

02 — The model, in contrast

A studio is structurally different from a venture fund.

The differences aren’t cosmetic. They change who finds the idea, who owns it, who runs it, and who carries the upside.

  Traditional VC House of Ideas
Idea source Founder HoI research
Capital Yes Yes — angels to VCs
Operational support Limited Full — HoI runs ops
Mentorship Advisory Active, embedded
IP ownership Founder HoI
Fellow’s upside Via equity Stipend now + equity option post-college
Talent filter Open Premier institutions only

03 — The Filter

The filter is the differentiation. Not arbitrary, not snobbery — positioning.

HoI works exclusively with students from IITs, NITs, and a defined set of India’s top institutions. The choice is deliberate: just as certain consulting firms and investment banks build their identity around where they hire from, HoI builds its identity around who it builds with. The filter creates two things — a consistently high talent bar across every venture, and a brand that means something in the market.

04 — The Vision

A new class of ventures — not born from accident, but from deliberate research and the best young talent in the country.

Over time, the HoI portfolio becomes a body of work: a set of companies built methodically, staffed by exceptional people, and backed by a studio that knows how to take an idea from zero to one.

The goal is not to be the biggest fund. The goal is to be the most deliberate one.
