House of Ideas03 — The Gap
The problem

Two halves of the same gap.

Talent without a path

India's IITs, NITs and top colleges produce tens of thousands of exceptional engineers and thinkers every year. Most walk into placements — not because they don't want to build, but because building feels structureless, underfunded, and lonely.

Ideas without operators

At the same time, thoroughly researched, validated ideas sit unbuilt across AI, HealthTech, EdTech, AgriTech, FinTech and ClimaTech — because no one has the capital, the structure, and the right people in place at the same time.

House of Ideas sits in that gap — and closes both sides at once.

The gap is not talent. The gap is structured opportunity.03

Idea research

House of Ideas09 — Ownership
IP & ownership structure

The IP sits at the studio. That is the model.

All intellectual property developed within HoI-backed ventures is owned by House of Ideas. This is foundational — it gives the studio:

—

Continuity

Ventures survive Fellow transitions.

—

Control

Directional decisions without founder conflict.

—

Portfolio value

IP held at studio level, appreciating with each venture.

Fellow compensation

Fellows are not employees. They earn a monthly stipend during the fellowship, and a pre-agreed equity stake — activated if and when they return post-graduation to run the venture full-time. The equity framework is agreed upfront and documented in the Fellowship Agreement.

Continuity · Control · Portfolio value.09
