House of Ideas02 — The Opportunity
The opportunity for your campus

Your most capable students want to build. Few have a real way to.

Building usually means leaving the safety of a placement for a blank page, no capital, and no support.

House of Ideas offers a third option: a researched, funded venture with mentorship and operational support — that a student can run during college, without stepping away from their degree.

A legitimate alternative to placement, not a replacement for the degree.02
House of Ideas03 — What We Are
What House of Ideas is

A venture studio — not a fund, not an accelerator.

We research and validate high-potential ideas, fund them, and recruit exceptional students to build them.

The student — the Fellow — receives the idea, the capital, a step-by-step roadmap, and active mentorship. We run the operations. They drive the product.

We bring the idea, the capital, the plan, and the mentorship.03
