House of Ideas05 — Two Ways In
Two ways to apply

Take an idea we've researched — or bring your own.

Track A — Build an HoI Idea

Explore the ideas on the HoI platform. Each comes with a research brief, a competitive landscape summary, and a phased execution roadmap. If one resonates, apply to take it forward.

Track B — Bring your own problem

Already working on something, but limited by structure, capital, or scale? Apply to bring your venture into the HoI portfolio. If it's a fit, we co-shape the direction and provide the resources to move.

In both tracks, IP is held by House of Ideas — this is what lets a venture continue regardless of who builds it at any stage.

Build an HoI Idea, or bring your own.05

House of Ideas07 — Selection
The selection process

We want to understand how you think.

01

Application

A structured written application, specific to the idea or track you've chosen. We care how you think, not just what you've done.

02

Problem deep-dive

A focused conversation with the HoI team — the idea, the market, your approach. No presentations required.

03

Decision

A go/no-go within a defined window. If selected, you receive a Fellowship Agreement covering stipend, IP, equity framework, and the post-college option.

We filter for mindset, not credentials.07
