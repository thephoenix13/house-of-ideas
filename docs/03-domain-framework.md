# House of Ideas — Domain Framework

---

## Purpose of This Document

This framework defines the domains HoI researches and sources ideas from. It serves two purposes:

1. **Internal** — Guides the HoI research team on where to look, what signals to track, and how to evaluate potential ideas before listing them on the platform.
2. **Technical** — Serves as the input specification for the HoI Idea Discovery Agent, which crawls the web for emerging opportunities within these domains.

---

## Domain Selection Criteria

Before a domain is added to the active research list, it must meet the following criteria:

- **Market timing** — Is the market ready, or is it still 5+ years out?
- **Structural tailwind** — Is there a regulatory, technological, or demographic shift accelerating this space?
- **India relevance** — Does this problem exist meaningfully in the Indian context?
- **Execution feasibility** — Can a small, focused team make meaningful early progress with the capital available?
- **IP potential** — Is there defensible value that HoI can protect and build on?

## Active Research Domains

### 1. AI & Intelligent Systems
Ideas at the intersection of artificial intelligence and real-world workflow automation. Not AI for AI's sake — AI applied to specific, high-friction problems.

**Sub-domains:**
- AI agents for enterprise workflows
- Conversational and voice AI
- Document intelligence and processing
- Predictive systems (maintenance, risk, demand)
- AI for HR, recruitment, and talent management

**Signal sources:** arXiv, Hugging Face, AI research blogs, enterprise software forums, Y Combinator batch releases

---

### 2. EdTech & Learning Infrastructure
The business of learning is broken in India and globally. Not consumer tutoring apps — infrastructure, credentialing, and institutional tools.

**Sub-domains:**
- AI-assisted assessments and proctoring
- Skill verification and credentialing
- Faculty and institutional tooling
- Corporate learning and upskilling platforms
- Vernacular and accessibility-first education tools

**Signal sources:** NASSCOM reports, UGC/AICTE announcements, global EdTech VC deal flow, LinkedIn Learning trends

---

### 3. HealthTech & BioTech
Healthcare in India is under-digitised, under-indexed on preventive care, and ripe for infrastructure-level disruption.

**Sub-domains:**
- Family and preventive health management
- Mental health tooling
- Hospital and clinic operations software
- AI diagnostics and decision support
- Rural health delivery infrastructure

**Signal sources:** ICMR data, NHA (National Health Authority) updates, global health VC reports, WHO digital health briefs

---

### 4. FinTech & Financial Infrastructure
Beyond payments — the layer above and below where money moves.

**Sub-domains:**
- Credit access for underserved segments
- Insurance discovery and claims automation
- Wealth management for first-time investors
- B2B payment infrastructure and reconciliation
- Embedded finance for non-financial platforms

**Signal sources:** RBI circulars, SEBI updates, Tracxn FinTech deal flow, CB Insights India reports

---

### 5. AgriTech & Rural Infrastructure
One of India's largest sectors by employment, one of the most under-served by technology.

**Sub-domains:**
- Crop monitoring and precision farming
- Cold chain and supply chain logistics
- Farm-to-market platforms
- Agricultural credit and insurance
- Input procurement and price discovery

**Signal sources:** ICAR research, Ministry of Agriculture bulletins, AgFunder reports, Krishi Vigyan Kendra programs

---

### 6. Climate Tech & Sustainability
The structural tailwind here is regulatory and generational. Early-stage infrastructure that scales as mandates tighten.

**Sub-domains:**
- Carbon tracking and reporting for SMEs
- EV infrastructure and fleet management
- Waste management and circular economy platforms
- Green building and energy management
- Water and resource efficiency tools

**Signal sources:** MoEFCC (India) updates, IRENA reports, Climate tech VC deal flow, IEA data

---

### 7. Enterprise SaaS & B2B Tools
India's SME and mid-market is chronically underserved by software built for them — not adapted from the West.

**Sub-domains:**
- Operations and workflow management
- Compliance and regulatory tooling
- Supply chain visibility
- HR and people management for growing teams
- Customer and field service management

**Signal sources:** Bessemer State of the Cloud, G2 category growth data, Salesforce ecosystem gaps, SaaS deal flow reports

---

### 8. GovTech & Civic Infrastructure
Government as a platform. Tooling that helps institutions serve citizens better, faster, and more transparently.

**Sub-domains:**
- Public grievance and feedback systems
- Municipal operations and asset management
- Education and health data infrastructure
- Tender and procurement transparency tools
- Identity and document management

**Signal sources:** Digital India program updates, NITI Aayog publications, State government RFPs, Open Government Data portal

---

### 9. Legal Tech & Compliance
India's legal and compliance layer is paper-heavy, slow, and expensive. There is significant whitespace for tooling.

**Sub-domains:**
- Contract drafting and review automation
- Regulatory compliance tracking
- RFP and bid response automation
- Legal research and case analysis
- MSME compliance tools

**Signal sources:** Bar Council of India, MCA filings, Legal VC deal flow, LegalZoom-equivalent gap analysis

---

### 10. Consumer & Community Platforms
Selective inclusion — only ideas with a clear network effect or behaviour change mechanism.

**Sub-domains:**
- Niche professional communities
- Creator economy infrastructure
- Hyperlocal marketplaces
- Wellness and mental health consumer apps
- Social commerce for Tier 2/3 India

**Signal sources:** App Store / Play Store trend data, Reddit/Twitter signal, Product Hunt launches, Sensor Tower data

---

## How Ideas Are Evaluated Within Domains

Every idea that passes domain-level relevance is assessed on six dimensions before listing:

| Dimension | What We Ask |
|---|---|
| Problem clarity | Is the pain point specific and verifiable? |
| Market size | Is there a defensible TAM? |
| Timing | Why now? What's changed? |
| Execution path | Can a small team make progress in 6 months? |
| Competitive landscape | Who else is doing this, and what's the gap? |
| India fit | Does this work in the Indian regulatory and cultural context? |

Ideas that pass this filter are listed on the platform with a full research brief and phased execution roadmap.

---

## Domain Review Cadence

- **Quarterly** — Full domain review. Add or remove domains based on market signals.
- **Monthly** — Signal sweep within active domains. Flag emerging sub-domains.
- **Ongoing** — Idea Discovery Agent runs continuously, surfaces candidates for human review.
