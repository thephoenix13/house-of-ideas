# Research Agent

---

## What It Does

The Research Agent runs structured research on demand — market sizing, competitor analysis, industry trend reports, customer segment profiles, and technology landscape briefs — and delivers outputs structured for decision-making, not just reading.

---

## The Problem It Solves

Good decisions need good information. Founders know this, and they also know that research takes time — time spent reading reports, synthesising data, and building a picture of a market that may or may not be accurate.

The Research Agent compresses that process. A founder who needs to understand the competitive landscape before a sales meeting, or who wants a market size estimate before committing to a new segment, gets a structured brief in the time it used to take to even find the right sources.

---

## How It Works

The founder submits a research request — a question, a market, a competitor, a technology. The agent determines the right research approach, pulls from relevant sources, synthesises the findings, and structures the output around the decision the founder is trying to make.

Every output flags its sources, its confidence level, and the assumptions embedded in any estimate. The goal is not to provide the illusion of certainty — it is to give the founder the best available picture, clearly.

---

## What It Handles

- Market sizing — TAM, SAM, SOM with methodology explained
- Competitor landscape analysis — positioning, pricing, product, traction
- Industry and sector trend briefs
- Customer segment profiling — demographics, behaviour, needs, willingness to pay
- Technology and tool evaluation reports
- Regulatory environment overviews for new markets
- Academic and industry research summarisation
- Pre-meeting briefing documents

---

## Output

A structured research brief built around the specific question or decision the founder is facing. The output is designed to be read in ten minutes and acted on — not filed away.
