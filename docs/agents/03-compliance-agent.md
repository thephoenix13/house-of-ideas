# Compliance Agent

---

## What It Does

The Compliance Agent tracks every regulatory obligation that applies to a HoI venture, ensures that nothing is missed or late, and prepares the documentation founders need to stay on the right side of the law — without requiring a full-time legal or finance hire to manage it.

---

## The Problem It Solves

Compliance in India is not optional, and it is not simple. A private limited company faces obligations across company law, tax, labour law, and — depending on the sector — additional regulatory requirements. Missing a filing or deadline carries penalties that are disproportionate to the oversight, and in a tight community like IIT alumni networks, a compliance failure can damage reputation in ways that take years to repair.

Most early-stage founders do not have a chartered accountant, company secretary, or legal counsel on payroll. The Compliance Agent fills that gap for the routine work.

---

## How It Works

The agent maintains a compliance calendar specific to each venture — built from its incorporation details, sector, employee count, and registered office location. It tracks deadlines, sends alerts before they are due, and prepares checklists of what needs to be filed, by whom, and with which authority.

For non-standard situations — a regulatory change, a sector-specific requirement, or a cross-border transaction — it flags the issue for human legal review rather than attempting to handle it autonomously.

---

## What It Handles

- MCA filings — annual returns, financial statements, director KYC
- Company secretarial reminders — board meetings, statutory registers
- GST registration, returns, and reconciliation reminders
- TDS and advance tax payment alerts
- PF and ESIC registration and monthly remittance reminders
- Shops and Establishments Act compliance by state
- Sector-specific regulatory tracking
- Compliance status summaries and gap reports

---

## Output

A live compliance calendar with upcoming deadlines, a status dashboard showing what is filed and what is pending, and pre-prepared checklists for each filing event. Founders always know where they stand.
