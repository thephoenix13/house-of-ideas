# Investor Relations Agent

---

## What It Does

The Investor Relations Agent manages the rhythm and substance of communication between a HoI venture and its investors — drafting updates, maintaining the data room, tracking investor asks, and ensuring that the venture never goes dark.

---

## The Problem It Solves

Investors who do not hear from a company regularly assume the worst. Consistent, well-structured communication builds trust, keeps investors warm for follow-on rounds, and creates a paper trail of progress that matters when it is time to raise again.

Most founders know this and still under-communicate — not because they do not care, but because writing investor updates is time-consuming and easy to defer when the business is demanding attention. The Investor Relations Agent removes the friction so that communication happens on schedule, every time.

---

## How It Works

The agent follows a monthly and quarterly cadence. At the end of each month, it pulls together data from across the venture — financial performance, key metrics, hiring updates, product milestones — and drafts a structured investor update. The founder reviews, edits if needed, and sends.

It also maintains the data room — keeping documents current, tracking which investors have access to what, and flagging when key documents are out of date. When investors ask questions, the agent drafts responses for founder review.

---

## What It Handles

- Monthly investor update drafts (highlights, metrics, asks)
- Quarterly business review decks
- Data room organisation and access management
- Investor query tracking and response drafts
- Cap table summary generation
- Follow-on fundraise documentation
- Investor meeting scheduling and prep briefs
- Thank-you and follow-up correspondence after meetings

---

## Output

A monthly investor update ready for review and dispatch, a current and organised data room, and a complete log of investor communications and outstanding asks. No investor is left without an update; no ask goes untracked.
