# Idea Discovery Agent

---

## What It Does

The Idea Discovery Agent is the starting point of every HoI venture. Before a single line of code is written or a single rupee is deployed, this agent does the upstream work — scanning domains, mapping gaps, evaluating ideas, and producing structured venture briefs that the HoI team can act on.

It is not a brainstorming tool. It is a research and evaluation engine that operates continuously, surfaces opportunities systematically, and stress-tests ideas before they reach any human decision-maker.

---

## The Problem It Solves

Finding good startup ideas is not a random process, but most people treat it like one. The Idea Discovery Agent imposes structure on that process. It ensures that HoI's venture pipeline is always full, that ideas are evaluated on consistent criteria, and that no opportunity in a target domain goes unexamined.

---

## How It Works

The agent operates in three stages:

**1. Domain Scanning**
It monitors activity across target domains — tracking market signals, funding announcements, regulatory changes, technology shifts, and search trend data. It builds a continuously updated picture of where movement is happening.

**2. Idea Generation and Structuring**
From the domain signals it identifies, the agent generates specific startup ideas. Each idea is structured into a brief: the problem being solved, the customer, the market size, the business model, and the execution angle.

**3. Evaluation and Scoring**
Each idea is scored against a set of criteria — market size, founder-market fit, defensibility, timing, and known failure patterns in the space. Ideas that pass the threshold are flagged for HoI team review.

---

## What It Handles

- Domain trend analysis and opportunity mapping
- Startup idea generation from market signals
- Structured idea briefs (problem, customer, market, model)
- Scoring and ranking against evaluation criteria
- Competitive landscape snapshots for each idea
- Execution roadmap scaffolding for shortlisted ideas
- Failure pattern analysis and risk flagging

---

## Output

The agent produces a structured output for each evaluated idea — a document the HoI team can pick up directly for internal review or use as the foundation for a full investment memo.
