# HR Agent

---

## What It Does

The HR Agent handles the administrative and process side of hiring and people management for a HoI venture — from the moment a role is defined to the point where a new hire is fully onboarded and operational.

---

## The Problem It Solves

Hiring is one of the highest-leverage decisions a founder makes, and it is also one of the most process-heavy. Writing job descriptions, coordinating interviews, sending offer letters, running background checks, setting up payroll — none of this requires founder-level judgment, but all of it consumes founder-level time.

For a Fellow running a HoI venture with a small team, a bad hire or a slow hire can stall progress for months. The HR Agent compresses the administrative overhead so that the founder's energy goes into the judgment calls — who to hire, not how to hire them.

---

## How It Works

The agent manages the full hiring workflow from role definition to onboarding. The founder describes the role they need to fill and the profile they are looking for. The agent drafts the job description, sets up the tracking pipeline, coordinates scheduling, and generates the paperwork at each stage.

For ongoing people management, the agent tracks leave, surfaces payroll reminders, and maintains a record of each team member's documentation.

---

## What It Handles

- Job description drafting and posting coordination
- Candidate pipeline tracking across stages
- Interview scheduling and confirmation emails
- Offer letter and appointment letter generation
- Background verification coordination
- Onboarding checklists and first-week schedules
- Employee documentation and record maintenance
- Leave tracking and payroll reminders
- Exit formalities and documentation

---

## Output

A structured hiring pipeline with status for each candidate, generated documents at each stage (JD, offer letter, appointment letter, onboarding guide), and a current view of team composition and pending HR tasks.
