# House of Ideas — Concept Document

---

## The Idea in One Line

House of Ideas is a venture studio that researches high-potential ideas, funds them, and recruits exceptional students from India's premier institutions to build them.

---

## The Problem We're Solving

India produces tens of thousands of brilliant engineers and thinkers from IITs, NITs, and top colleges every year. Most of them walk into placements — not because they don't want to build, but because building feels structureless, underfunded, and lonely.

On the other side, there are ideas worth building — thoroughly researched, validated, and ready for execution — sitting without the right people to take them forward.

House of Ideas sits in that gap.

---

## What House of Ideas Is

House of Ideas (HoI) is a venture studio.

We are not a traditional fund that waits for founders to pitch us. We do the upstream work: research domains, identify ideas with genuine market potential, build the execution roadmap, and then find the right person to run it.

We bring:
- The idea (researched and validated)
- The capital ($100,000 – $500,000 per venture)
- The plan (step-by-step execution roadmap)
- The mentorship (active guidance from the HoI team)
- The infrastructure (HoI runs operations)

The Fellow brings:
- Execution capability
- Domain instinct
- The hunger to build something real

---

## How It Works

### Step 1 — Idea Research
HoI continuously researches markets, emerging trends, and whitespace opportunities across defined domains. Every idea listed on the platform comes with a validated hypothesis, a competitive landscape assessment, and a phased execution roadmap.

### Step 2 — The Platform
Ideas are listed on the HoI platform. Students and builders from premier institutions can explore, read the brief, and apply to take one forward. Alternatively, if someone is already working on a problem and needs structure, capital, or scale — they can apply to bring their idea into the HoI portfolio.

### Step 3 — Selection
Applications are reviewed by the HoI team. Selection is rigorous. We look for intellectual sharpness, execution mindset, and genuine commitment — not just credentials.

### Step 4 — The Build Phase
Selected Fellows work on the venture during their time in college. They receive a stipend, active mentoring, access to the execution roadmap, and direct support from the HoI team. HoI manages the operational side of the venture while the Fellow drives the product and customer thinking.

### Step 5 — Post-College
When the Fellow graduates, they have the option to return to the venture full-time at a pre-agreed equity stake. This is their real entry into the venture — as a full-time operator with skin in the game.

---

## What Makes HoI Different

**The filter is the differentiation.**

HoI works exclusively with students from IITs, NITs, and a defined set of India's top institutions. This is a deliberate positioning choice — not an arbitrary gate. Just as certain consulting firms and investment banks build their identity around where they hire from, HoI builds its identity around who it builds with.

This creates two things:
1. A consistently high talent bar across every venture in the portfolio
2. A brand that means something in the market — to fellows, to partners, and eventually to the ventures themselves

Beyond the filter:
- We don't wait for founders to have ideas. We bring the idea.
- We don't just write cheques. We build alongside.
- We don't disappear after funding. We run operations.

## The Studio Model vs. Traditional VC

| | Traditional VC | House of Ideas |
|---|---|---|
| Idea source | Founder | HoI Research |
| Capital | Yes | Yes ($100k–$500k) |
| Operational support | Limited | Full (HoI runs ops) |
| Mentorship | Advisory | Active, embedded |
| IP ownership | Founder | HoI |
| Fellow's upside | Via equity | Stipend now + equity option post-college |
| Talent filter | Open | Premier institutions only |

---

## IP and Ownership

All intellectual property developed within HoI-backed ventures is owned by House of Ideas. This is foundational to the studio model — it ensures continuity, protects the venture if a Fellow exits, and allows HoI to maintain and evolve each portfolio company over time.

Fellows are not employees. They are builders with a genuine path to equity when they return post-college. The equity stake is agreed upon at the point of selection and formalised at graduation.

---

## The Vision

HoI is building a new class of ventures — not born from accident or luck, but from deliberate research, structured execution, and the best young talent in the country.

Over time, the HoI portfolio becomes a body of work: a set of companies built methodically, staffed by exceptional people, and backed by a studio that knows how to take an idea from zero to one.

The goal is not to be the biggest fund. The goal is to be the most deliberate one.
