The Idea Catalogue.

A growing set of researched, validated ideas across 10 active domains. Each comes with a hypothesis, a competitive landscape assessment, and a phased execution roadmap — the kind of brief a Fellow can pick up and run with.

The catalogue is not yet public. To explore listed ideas, request access below.

01 — How an idea makes the catalogue

Six dimensions. Every idea is tested against all of them.

Ideas surface continuously from our research operation. Only those that pass a structured six-dimension review reach the catalogue.

01

Problem clarity

Is the pain point specific and verifiable, or vague and aspirational?

02

Market size

Is there a defensible TAM, or is this a niche dressed up as an opportunity?

03

Timing

Why now? What has changed in regulation, technology, or behaviour?

04

Execution path

Can a small team make meaningful progress in six months with early capital?

05

Competitive landscape

Who else is here, and is there a real gap, or a crowded space?

06

India fit

Does this work inside the Indian regulatory and cultural context, not just in theory?
