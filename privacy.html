Privacy Policy

This is a draft privacy policy for House of Ideas. It will be replaced with a counsel-reviewed version before public launch. The summary below describes our current intentions.

What we collect

  • Contact information you provide when you email us, book a call, or apply to the Fellowship (your name, email, institution, year, and any context you share).
  • Basic, anonymous traffic data via privacy-respecting analytics (page views, country, referrer). No tracking cookies, no advertising pixels.

How we use it

  • To respond to your enquiries and process Fellowship applications.
  • To send you communications you have asked for (and only those).
  • To understand which parts of the site are useful, in aggregate.

What we don’t do

  • We don’t sell your information.
  • We don’t share it with advertisers.
  • We don’t use third-party tracking beyond a single privacy-respecting analytics tool.

Your rights

You can ask us to delete any personal information we hold on you at any time. Write to us via our contact form with the subject “Data request”.

Contact

For any privacy-related question, use our contact form.

This policy is currently a working draft and will be formalised before House of Ideas operates as a registered legal entity. Final terms may differ.
