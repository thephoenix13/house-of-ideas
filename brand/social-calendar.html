House of Ideas is a venture studio.

We don’t wait for founders to pitch us. We do the upstream work ourselves: research the domain, find an idea with real market potential, build the execution roadmap — and then find the right person to run it.

Week 3 · Jun 15–21 — The filter

Mon Jun 15LinkedInanchor · textP1 Manifesto

Post / image

House of IdeasThe filter is the differentiation.

Caption

We work only with students from India’s premier institutions — the IITs, the NITs, and a defined set of others. This is deliberate, not arbitrary.

It isn’t about prestige. It’s about a specific kind of raw material: people who think rigorously, learn fast, and aren’t afraid of ambiguity. The filter is a structural advantage no fund can replicate.

The filter is the differentiation.

Tue Jun 16XthreadP1 Manifesto

ThreadThe talent thesis.

Caption — thread

  1. Every venture studio competes on the same thing: the quality of the operators it can attract.
  2. Most compete in an open market. We start from a filtered one.
  3. Students from India’s premier institutions — rigorous, fast, comfortable with hard problems.
  4. We don’t ask them to also be the idea-generator and fundraiser and ops lead on day one.
  5. We hand them a de-risked idea and let them do the part they’re built for: execute.
  6. The filter is the differentiation.
